    a) $\mathbf{P}\left(\cap_{i=1}^{n} A_{i}\right)=1$

    c) $\mathbf{P}\left(\cap_{i=1}^{n} A_{i}\right)=\frac{1}{n}$
    d) $\mathbf{P}\left(\cap_{i=1}^{n} A_{i}\right)=n$
