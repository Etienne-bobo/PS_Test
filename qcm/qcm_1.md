
    Question: In a town, there are four bakeries. Each one of them has to close one day a week. How many different ways are there to assign a weekly closing day to each bakery?
    Answer Choices:
    a) 7^4=2401
    b) 4^7=16384
    c) 4!=24
    d) 7×4=28
    Solution: The correct answer is a) 7^4=2401. For each bakery, there are 7 possible choices for the closing day, leading to a total of 7^4=2401 different ways to assign a weekly closing day to each bakery.
